Who We Are

Anarchist

38SOG is operated on a non-heirarchical consensus-based governance model. There are no set leaders. Leadership in the field is determined by present need and experience, rather than on fixed roles.

Colonial

The Colonial Army prides itself on being welcoming and friendly. 38SOG is proud to be part of these efforts. As such, 38SOG is strictly a Colonial group.

Operations

38SOG was formed to plan, coordinate, and execute large-scale, collaborative, multi-clan operations. We aim to provide the right tools to the right people in the right place at the right time.

What We Do

Observe - Advise - Mobilize

The Right Tools

Logistics is the backbone of Foxhole. Everything from tanks to rifles to respawns is prepared and delivered by players. The largest part of providing the right tools to the right people in the right place at the right time is having those tools to begin with

The Right People

38SOG knows and works closely with outside players. Our most successful operations never involve 38SOG going solo. Our strength lies in inviting other players and clans to fill out the roles in our operations, allowing us to be the fulcrum for a much larger lever.

The Right Place

Scouting is key to any operation. It's the difference between landing a ship on a watchtower-covered beach straight into concrete, and crossing a quiet border to walk up to an undefend town. 38SOG scouts to identify decayed defenses, unbuilt map sections, and targets of opportunity.

The Right Time

38SOG is primarily NA timezone, but has a substantial contingent of Australian and Kiwi players. We plan our operations at least 24 hours in advance, to give everyone time to clear their schedule and be ready.

Small Unit Activities

38SOG isn't always trying to move the whole map. We spend a lot of time on small-unit activities, especially those that reward patience and preparation

Artillery

To shoot artillery in Foxhole is easy; to do it well is hard. 38SOG members bring patience and discipline to artillery fire missions, in addition to hundreds of shells, allowing us to operate effectively and continuously.

Anti-Armor

Patience is a recurring theme in 38SOG's small unit gameplay, and it shines in our anti-armor work. Anti-tank weapons don't do well against structures or enemy troops, but sitting and waiting for our chance to strike means we're in position when the enemy finally makes their move.
